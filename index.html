Simulation | MATLAB

Photon Paths Near a Black Hole

This project took a difficult physics topic and turned it into a plotted simulation that can be inspected quickly. I like it because the output does not just prove the math runs. It makes the behavior easier to explain.

  • What I built A MATLAB model for photon capture, escape, and critical trajectory behavior near a black hole.
  • What the main still shows Multiple path families in a single frame, which makes the orbital behavior readable at a glance.
  • Why it matters It demonstrates numerical thinking, visualization, and the ability to communicate a hard concept clearly.
