How I work

My strongest projects blend product sense with engineering depth.

  • Clarify the goal. I like turning a vague brief into a concrete plan with good tradeoffs and obvious milestones.
  • Make the hard parts visible. The riskiest states, edge cases, and data flows get designed early, not patched in at the end.
  • Ship a credible first version. I prefer launching something polished enough to build trust immediately.
  • Leave the system better. I aim for handoff docs, patterns, and cleanup that help the next sprint move faster.
